CALABARZON


CALABARZON (/ká-lɑ-bɑr-zon/) is one of the regions of the Philippines. It is designated as Region IV-A and its regional center is Calamba City in Laguna. The region is composed of five provinces, namely: Cavite, Laguna, Batangas, Rizal, and Quezon; whose names form the acronym CALABARZON. The region is also more formally known 9/22/2014 as Southern Tagalog Mainland.
Angono in Rizal Province is considered as the “Art Capital of the Philippines” because of the towns rich artistic expressions that include music, painting, sculpting and folk arts.
