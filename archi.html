ARCHITECTURES

San Juan Bautista Church

Liliw Laguna is also known for San Juan Bautista Church, Baroque architecture composed of red bricks which is one of the beautiful churches in the area.

Nagcarlan Underground Cemetery

This Baroque cemetery was built in 1845 by the Franciscan Missionaries, This Underground Cemetery has circular wall structure with iron-gate to the red-tiled wherein the tombstones are embedded on the brick walls. It’s the only underground cemetery in the country and its one of the national landmarks

Saint Padre Pio Parish and Shrine

It is also a place for pilgrims and devotees. It is also a popular church to go to during “ Visita Iglesia” within the Holy Week Season.

Taal Basilica

It is more commonly referred to, as the biggest Catholic Church in Asia.

Imus Cathedral

Also known as the Cathedral of our Lady of Pillar, is one of Cavite’s historical tourist spots. It dates back to the Spanish years and since then, has been an important occasions and events.

Rizal Shrine

Laguna is also famous for being home to the country’s national hero, Jose Rizal. In Calamba, a shrine was erected in his memory, and is open to all interested parties who are curious about the polyglot’s humble origins.
