CRAFTS


KIPING


The town of Lucban, is famous for the Pahiyas Festival Celebrated on the 15th of May to honor San Isidro Labrador, the patron saint of farmers.

Kiping is made form ground glutinous rice that is thinly coated on mature leaves and steamed over low fire. A colorful leaf shaped glutinous rice craft in decorating Pahiyas Festival Celebration.


PIÑA FABRIC


Taal Batangas famous for producing embroidered piÑa fabric, made from the fibers of pineapple and woven into a costly fabric. These are used for piÑa barong, wedding gowns, and other formal attire. The burda, called caldado is painstalking made by three groups of people the designer, the embroider and the cutter.


BIG PAPER MACHE


Rizal Province is also known for its higantes. The higantes are big paper mache figures of humans that represent farmers and fishermen. The earliest known “higantes” are composed of father, mother, and child measuring between 7 to 10 feet. These are traditionally paraded on the 22nd and 23rd of November in Angono Rizal.


UGU BIGYAN POTTERY


Augusto Ugu Bigyan is one of the famous potters in the country. His artistic works are displayed in different resorts, hotels, restaurants and houses of prominent personalities. Ugu Bigyan is located in Lusacan, Tiaong, Quezon which serves are place for workshops, showroom and restaurant in which serve delectable local dishes for he has a heart for cooking also.
